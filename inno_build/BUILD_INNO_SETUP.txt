🔧 BUILD WITH INNO SETUP:

REQUIREMENT:
- Inno Setup 6.x must be installed on your system
  Download: https://jrsoftware.org/isdl.php

STEPS TO BUILD:

1. Open RR_BILLING_PRO_v2.3.iss with Inno Setup Compiler

2. Click "Build" → "Compile"
   The installer will be created in Output\ folder:
   Output/RR_BILLING_PRO_v2.3_Setup.exe

3. Distribute RR_BILLING_PRO_v2.3_Setup.exe to users
